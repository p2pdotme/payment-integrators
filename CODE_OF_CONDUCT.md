# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its code of conduct.

The full text, including the standards we expect contributors to uphold and the categories of behavior that are not tolerated, is maintained at the link above. By participating in this project (issues, PRs, discussions, or any other community space) you agree to abide by it.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, report it to the maintainers at:

**dev@p2p.me**

Reports will be reviewed and acted on promptly. Reporter identity is kept confidential.

This is the same shared alias used in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) — it reaches the maintainer team. When a `@p2pdotme/core` group is formalised in GitHub, enforcement will move there.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they consider appropriate, up to and including removing comments, closing issues or PRs, and banning contributors from the project. The [Contributor Covenant's enforcement guidelines](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines) are the reference framework.
